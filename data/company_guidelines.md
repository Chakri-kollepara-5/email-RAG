# Company Email Tone Guidelines

- Always use a polite, respectful and neutral tone.
- Never blame, threaten, or accuse the recipient.
- Focus on solutions instead of complaints.
- Avoid emotional or aggressive wording.
- Keep sentences clear, concise, and professional.
- Do not promise refunds or financial commitments without verification.
- Avoid slang, sarcasm, or informal language.
- Show empathy when responding to complaints.
- Maintain professionalism in every sentence.
- Keep the message objective, factual, and calm.
